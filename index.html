Welcome!

こんにちは！

Embarking on a high school exchange trip from New Zealand to Japan is an exhilarating journey that promises a fusion of cultural immersion, educational enrichment, and unforgettable experiences. As the students eagerly anticipate their adventure, they are filled with a sense of curiosity and excitement about the unfamiliar yet captivating land they are about to explore.

But the exchange isn't just about sightseeing and sampling local delicacies. The students also have the opportunity to attend classes at a Japanese high school, where they gain firsthand insight into the Japanese education system and forge friendships with their Japanese peers. Through shared experiences in the classroom and on the sports field, bonds are formed that transcend language and cultural barriers.

About Suginami High

Suginami High School, located in the heart of Tokyo, Japan, stands as a beacon of academic excellence and cultural vibrancy. Renowned for its innovative teaching methods and commitment to holistic education, Suginami High School cultivates a stimulating learning environment where students are encouraged to explore their passions and excel academically. With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty, the school offers a diverse range of academic and extracurricular opportunities, from rigorous STEM programs to immersive cultural exchange initiatives. Embracing the principles of respect, integrity, and collaboration, Suginami High School nurtures students to become compassionate, globally-minded citizens prepared to navigate an increasingly interconnected world.
